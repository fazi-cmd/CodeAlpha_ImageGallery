FAQ's

Find quick answers to common questions about Pixero

Is Pixero really free to use?

Yes! Pixero offers a vast collection of completely free images and videos. You can download and use them for personal and commercial projects without paying anything.

Do I need to credit the creators?

While not mandatory, we always appreciate when users credit the creators. It helps support their work and encourages them to contribute more content.

How can I contribute my own content?

You can upload your content using our Upload button in the top right corner. All submissions go through a quality review before being published.

What file formats do you support?

For images: JPG, PNG, WEBP. For videos: MP4, MOV, WEBM. Maximum file size is 50MB for images and 500MB for videos.

Can I use Pixero content for commercial projects?

Absolutely! All content on Pixero is licensed under Creative Commons Zero (CC0), which means you can use it freely for any purpose, including commercial projects.
